Frequently Asked

How long does a typical laptop repair take? expand_more
Most repairs completed within 3 hours; simple fixes like screen or battery replacements often done in 30–60 minutes. Complex problems may take longer depending on parts availability.
Do you offer a warranty and an invoice/bill? expand_more
Yes, we offer an assured warranty on all repair types. You will receive an Invoice ID and a digital invoice, valid at any Robuzta store.
How do you ensure my data stays safe? expand_more
We never ask for your passwords or OTPs. If device access is required for hardware testing, it is strictly performed in your presence to assure complete data security.
How can I trust that the spare parts used are original? expand_more
All our parts are procured from OEM-approved suppliers. We show you the new parts, including serial numbers, before replacement, and perform the fix only after your approval. Plus, you get your old parts back.
