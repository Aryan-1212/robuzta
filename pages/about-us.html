Our Story

2012

Inception & Experiments

Robuzta began with a simple curiosity: how do gadgets really work — and how can they be repaired better? The journey started in 2012 with hands-on experiments on early smartphones, driven by a deep curiosity for how devices function. This passion soon transformed into professional training, mastering diagnostics, repair processes, and quality standards.

2014

Axiom Technologies

By 2014, the first venture — Axiom Technologies — was launched, a multi-brand repair shop designed as a one-stop solution for smartphone users. Over the years, thousands of repairs built both technical mastery and a reputation for transparent, reliable service.

2020

Adapting to the Pandemic

When the pandemic reshaped how people lived and worked in 2020, the demand for laptops and tablets surged. Rising to the challenge, the team quickly expanded beyond mobiles into laptops, tablets, desktops, and more, adapting to the new digital-first lifestyle.

2021

Evolving to Robuzta Techlabs

In 2021, the brand evolved into Robuzta Techlabs — a name inspired by “robust,” symbolizing strength and reliability, and “Techlabs,” reflecting a commitment to precision and innovation. Today, Robuzta is a full-service, multi-brand repair network covering smartphones, laptops, tablets, smartwatches, AIO systems, and gaming PCs.

Now

A Decade of Precision

With multiple service centres, a dedicated laboratory, and a team of skilled engineers, Robuzta combines modern tools, strict parts verification, and rigorous quality checks. Over a decade of hands-on experience has shaped one guiding principle: repair with care, accuracy, and transparency. Every repair is backed by genuine parts, clear estimates, and warranty-protected service.
